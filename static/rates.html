 Rates

based on a standard 25-line page

 

Standard

(48-72 Hours)

$.40 per page

Rush

(24-48 Hours)

$.60 per page

Expedite

(Less than 24 hours)

$.80 per page

Additional Charges

An additional fee of $0.10/page may be charged for expert testimony* or extra messy** transcripts.

*Average of 3+ errors per page (charged at my discretion)

**Highly dense, technical transcripts (charged at my discretion)

Client Referral Discount

10% off your next job (up to 100 pages) upon receipt of first payment from referral.

Business Days

Sundays and major holidays (Thanksgiving, Christmas, and New Year’s Day) are not included as business days.

Rate is based on the time stamp of your email.

Jobs received after 7 pm PST count as if received at 8 am the following day.

 Invoicing & Payment

First-time clients or a one-time job will be invoiced upon completion of the job, with payment due in 15 days. All other clients will be billed on the 1st and 15th of each month, with payment due in 15 days. Late payments will be charged a 10% fee.

I accept payments via credit card (processed through PayPal) or check.
